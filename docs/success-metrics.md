# Success Metrics — Smart Onboarding

1. **Can a new starter identify their top 3 Day 1 priorities within the first minute of opening the app?**
   (Tests H1 — addresses P02/P05/P06's "too much info, no priority" pain point)

2. **Can a new starter tell, at a glance, what they've completed and what's next?**
   (Tests H2 — addresses P03/P06's "didn't know what to do next" pain point)

3. **Can a new starter find the correct, current version of a document without asking a colleague?**
   (Tests H3 — addresses the 4/6 participants who lost up to an hour to version confusion)

4. **Can a new starter identify who to contact for a specific task within 30 seconds?**
   (Tests H4 — addresses P02/P06's "didn't know who to ask" pain point)

5. **Do new starters rate role-specific content as more useful than generic training in a post-task survey?**
   (Tests H5 — addresses P02/P06/P04's "box-ticking" feedback)

6. **When shown an AI-generated answer, can users correctly identify its source and last-updated date?**
   (Tests H6 — addresses the universal AI-trust requirement across all 6 participants)
